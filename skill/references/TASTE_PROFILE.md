## Pattern Analysis — What The Favorites Share

### 1. FACE QUALITY (Most Important)
Every favorite has EXCELLENT face rendering:
- Clear, expressive eyes with visible catchlights
- Defined jawline and bone structure
- Subtle skin rendering (not flat, not over-detailed)
- The face is the HERO — everything else supports it

### 2. THE "PORTRAIT-PLUS" CROP
All favorites show head + upper chest. NOT just head. NOT waist-down.
- Sacred Gear (collar, pendant, pauldron) visible
- Upper outfit visible (collar, neckline, shoulder)
- NO hands visible
- Character fills ~70-75% of frame
- **This is the sweet spot — encode as default**

### 3. STRONG SILHOUETTE FEATURE
Every favorite has ONE bold hair/silhouette element:
- Talia: sleek twist with pearl streak
- Zeph/Fenris: wild swept silver hair
- Yuna: long flowing blue-black
- Nira/Luna: moody dark with subtle color
- Eldrian Faelen: pointed ears + coral hairpin
- **The hair/head silhouette is the primary differentiator at thumbnail size**

### 4. MOOD OVER DETAIL
The favorites feel MOODY, not detailed:
- Deep, rich backgrounds (not bright)
- Atmospheric lighting with color temperature shift
- The character exists in a MOOD, not just on a flat color
- Dark presets scored VERY high (57, 61 both favorites)

### 5. ONE GLOWING ELEMENT
Every favorite has exactly ONE thing that glows:
- Starlight Mark (always)
- Crystal in Sacred Gear
- Eyes (for Celestial/Awakened)
- **Never more than 2 glow sources — restraint creates focus**

### 6. COLOR RESTRAINT
Favorites use 4-5 colors maximum on the character:
- Skin tone
- Hair color
- Outfit base (usually dark)
- ONE accent (teal, blue, purple, ember)
- Sacred Gear metal (usually dark with one glow)
- **The less color, the more premium it feels**

### 7. SPECIFIC FACE EXPRESSIONS
NOT generic "cool unbothered" — each has PERSONALITY:
- Talia: knowing, slight warmth
- Nyx: dangerous calm
- Fenris: stoic, steady
- Luna: mysterious, distant
- Cyberpunk #64: young, slightly mischievous
- **Vary expressions MORE — not everyone should smirk the same way**

## What The NON-Favorites Got Wrong

Characters NOT in the favorites list tend to have:
- Too many competing visual elements (busy)
- Generic expression (same smirk on everyone)
- Too bright/saturated (loses premium feel)
- Flat lighting (no atmosphere)
- Hands visible awkwardly
- Hair that doesn't create a strong silhouette
- Background too bright or too warm

## The Golden Rules (Extracted from Favorites)

1. **Face is king** — spend 40% of prompt on face details
2. **Portrait-plus crop** — upper chest visible, NO hands, Sacred Gear shows
3. **One bold silhouette element** — usually hair or headgear
4. **Dark, moody backgrounds** — not bright. Deep navy, charcoal, plum, forest
5. **Maximum ONE glow source** besides the Starlight Mark
6. **4-5 colors on character** — restraint = premium
7. **Vary expressions** — each character should feel like a PERSON, not a template
8. **Atmosphere > detail** — the MOOD matters more than the number of traits
9. **Dark preset and cyberpunk preset scored highest** — the collection should lean into these
10. **Water + Dark origins are the taste bar** — Talia/Yuna/Nira/Luna represent peak quality

## Prompt Enhancement Rules

When generating, add these to the prompt based on this taste profile:

```
ATMOSPHERE: Rich, deep, moody. The character exists in controlled dramatic light,
not flat studio. Color temperature shift visible — warm on lit side, cool on shadow.
One element softly glows. Everything else is material and form.

FACE: The face is the hero of this image. Defined bone structure. Eyes with clear
catchlights and personality — NOT generic. This specific character has a specific
inner life visible through their expression. Subtle, not exaggerated.

RESTRAINT: Maximum 4-5 colors on the character. One accent color only. One glow
source only. Remove any trait that doesn't earn its place. If in doubt, simplify.
```

## The 4 Customer Segments (Real People, Real Pain)

### Segment 1: The Solo Creator Who Dreams
**Who:** Artist, musician, writer who wants to launch their first NFT collection
**Pain:** Has vision but no tech skills, no budget for developers, overwhelmed by tools
**Budget:** $0-500 total (this is a side project, not a business yet)
**Where they hang out:** Twitter/X, Discord NFT communities, YouTube tutorials, Reddit r/NFT
**What they'd pay for:**
- A tool that takes their art style and generates 1K-10K variants ($99-299)
- Smart contract deployment without coding ($49-99)
- A mint page they can customize ($29-49)
- A Discord bot that knows their collection ($19/mo)
**How they want to pay:** One-time for generation, monthly for ongoing services. Credit card. NOT crypto (ironic but true — most creators don't hold ETH for gas yet).

### Segment 2: The Established Project Wanting More
**Who:** NFT project with existing collection (1K-10K minted), active community, some revenue
**Pain:** Collection is static, engagement dropping, need utility/evolution/new drops
**Budget:** $1K-10K for meaningful upgrades
**Where they hang out:** NFT Twitter, Discord alpha channels, NFT NYC/conferences, Dune dashboards
**What they'd pay for:**
- Dynamic evolution engine for their existing collection ($500-2K setup + $99/mo)
- AI community agent that knows their lore ($99-299/mo)
- Companion collection generation ($1K-5K)
- Cross-collection mechanics consulting ($500-2K)
**How they want to pay:** Crypto (ETH/USDC) or wire transfer. They have treasury.

### Segment 3: The Studio/Agency
**Who:** Web3 studio that launches collections for clients (3-10 per year)
**Pain:** Each collection takes 3-6 months. Need to ship faster, cheaper, better.
**Budget:** $5K-50K per project (passed through to client)
**Where they hang out:** Web3 conferences, Telegram groups, LinkedIn, private Discord servers
**What they'd pay for:**
- White-label generation engine ($5K-10K license + revenue share)
- Custom Style Pack creation ($2K-5K per pack)
- Full-service collection launch ($10K-25K)
- Ongoing dynamic NFT infrastructure ($500-2K/mo)
**How they want to pay:** Invoice, wire transfer, annual contracts.

### Segment 4: The Community/DAO
**Who:** Existing community (gaming guild, creator collective, fan community) wanting branded PFPs
**Pain:** Want identity/belonging for members but don't want to become an "NFT project"
**Budget:** $500-5K for a community identity collection
**Where they hang out:** Their own Discord, Twitter, potentially Farcaster
**What they'd pay for:**
- 500-2K branded PFPs for community members ($499-1,999)
- Community bot that manages the collection ($29/mo)
- Holder verification for Discord roles ($19/mo)
- Simple mint page ($99 one-time)
**How they want to pay:** Community treasury (multisig), Stripe, sometimes crypto.
